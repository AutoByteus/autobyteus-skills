## Body

### Move 1 — [Claim headline]
- **Why this move exists:** how it advances the thesis
- **Claim:** …

- **Reasoning:** why the evidence supports the claim
- **Competing interpretation / tension:** what could challenge or complicate this move
- **So what:** implication / application

Write this move as a developed section, not a single paragraph.
The default shape is:
1. setup / framing paragraph,
2. evidence synthesis in prose with embedded citations,
3. reasoning paragraph(s) linking evidence to claim,
4. counterpoint / caveat paragraph when relevant,
5. implication / application paragraph.
