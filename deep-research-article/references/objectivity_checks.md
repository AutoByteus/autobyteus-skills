# Objectivity checks (before final article)

Run after logic/evidence gates to reduce hidden bias.

## Claim balance
- Does the article represent the strongest counter-position fairly?
- Are dissenting sources quoted at comparable specificity?

## Confidence honesty
- Do conclusions match evidence strength (not stronger)?
- Are uncertain areas clearly labeled as uncertain?

## Scope discipline
- Are claims limited to the studied population/context/time window?
- Are normative statements separated from factual statements?

## Language neutrality
- Remove loaded or leading wording where evidence is mixed.
- Avoid overstating causality when evidence is correlational.

## Traceability
- Can each major conclusion be traced back to source IDs in the ledger?
